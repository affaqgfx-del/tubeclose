About Us

Built for B2B pipeline, not vanity metrics.

TubeClose is a founder-led, done-for-you YouTube partner. We help funded SaaS companies, agencies, and B2B coaches turn YouTube into a dependable source of qualified calls.

Most B2B teams know YouTube matters, but they do not have the time, the system, or the conversion strategy to make it pay off. They post a few videos, see little traction, and move on, convinced YouTube "does not work" for their niche.

We started TubeClose to prove the opposite. Done right, a focused B2B channel becomes your best-performing pipeline channel: buyers find you while they are researching, trust builds before the first call, and demo requests arrive pre-sold.

So we built a done-for-you system around one metric that matters: qualified demo calls booked from your channel. Research, scripting, filming direction, editing, thumbnails, SEO, and reporting are all handled end-to-end. You record once a week; we handle the rest.

Pipeline first Every decision is made to book qualified calls, not chase views or subscribers.
Founder-led B2B buyers trust people. We put your founder's expertise at the center of every video.
Done-for-you From strategy to publishing, we own the process so your team stays focused.
Data-driven We track what matters and improve every video based on real performance.
